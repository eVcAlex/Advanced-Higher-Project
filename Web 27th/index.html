Mularkey: Titans taking it day by day with Mariota

Titans coach Mike Mularkey said Monday that he will take it day by day with quarterback Marcus Mariota, who suffered a hamstring injury in Sunday's 57-14 thrashing at the hands of the Texans.

Carr diagnosed with a transverse process fracture

Oakland Raiders quarterback Derek Carr suffered a transverse process fracture in his back Sunday and "there's a real good chance he'll miss this week," coach Jack Del Rio told reporters Monday.

Norman suffers fractured rib during Redskins' loss

The Washington Redskins suffered a blow to their defense on Monday night. Josh Norman suffered a fractured rib and made an early exit during the team's loss to the Kansas City Chiefs.
